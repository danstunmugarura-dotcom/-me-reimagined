The 12-Week Journey

Every week has a purpose.
Every driver builds on the last.

This is not a collection of modules. It is a sequential system. You cannot design a new version of yourself without first seeing the current one clearly.

PRE-PROGRAMME
Before Week 1
iPhilosophy Intake
A deep questionnaire that captures your archetype, your definition of success, your current reality, your challenges and your goals. This data becomes the personalised foundation of your entire journey.
DAY ONE
Live with Danston
Live Kick-Off — 2 Hours
Every cohort opens with a live Zoom session led personally by Danston. This is not a recording. This is the moment the transformation begins — together, in real time.
D1 — DISCOVER
Weeks 1 & 2
Who Are You, Really?
A forensic look at who you are beneath the labels, roles and expectations. Your patterns, your energy, your strengths, and your blind spots. Self-awareness at the deepest level before anything else is built.
D2 — DIAGNOSE
Weeks 3 & 4
What Is Causing What Is Happening?
Most people treat symptoms. Diagnose goes to the root. What is creating your current results? What identity-level belief is running beneath the surface? What strengths, if amplified first, would change everything?
D3 — DISCUSS
Weeks 5 & 6
The Honest Conversation
Priorities, projects, people, and process. The conversations you have been avoiding. The decisions you have been deferring. Named clearly. Decided honestly.
D4 — DESIGN
Weeks 7, 8 & 9
Reimagine the New You
First design the human. Then upgrade the performance. Structure, standards, systems, and simplicity — your complete personal operating system. Built intentionally. Not by accident.
D5 — DELIVER
Weeks 10, 11 & 12
Express the Reimagined Person
Embody. Execute. Evaluate. Evolve. Design creates possibility. Delivery creates reality. This is where the work becomes visible — in your choices, your presence, your relationships, and your results.
GRADUATION
Live with Danston
Live Graduation — 2 Hours
When you complete the 12 weeks, you join the next graduation Zoom with Danston personally. A celebration, a commitment, and a send-off into everything the reimagined you is now capable of.
