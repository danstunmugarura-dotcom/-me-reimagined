The 5D Drivers

Five drivers.
Twelve weeks.

Each driver builds on the last. Work through them in sequence. The transformation is in the sequence.

01
Driver 1
Discover
Weeks 1–2
Who are you, really? Not the CV version. Not the LinkedIn version. The actual version — as evidenced by behaviour, patterns, and results. A forensic look at the current self before anything else is built.
02
Driver 2
Diagnose
Weeks 3–4
What is causing what is happening? Most people treat symptoms. Diagnose goes to the root — the identity-level belief running beneath the surface of your results.
03
Driver 3
Discuss
Weeks 5–6
The conversations you have been avoiding. The truth you have not yet said. Discuss is where the honest work becomes real — in your relationships, your leadership, and your business.
04
Driver 4
Design
Weeks 7–9
First design the human. Then upgrade the performance. Your Reimagined Identity Statement. Your 90-day commitment. Your new operating standard — chosen, not inherited.
05
Driver 5
Deliver
Weeks 10–12
The reimagined person, in the real world. Deliver is where the work meets the room — your business, your leadership, your relationships. Consistently. This is the transformation made permanent.
